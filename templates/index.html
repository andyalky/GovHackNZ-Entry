Building Better Communities

We combine open data from government agencies (such as Stats NZ) with third-party APIs (such as Facebook and Foursquare) to generate an in-depth picture of a neighbourhood's economic and social health.

Our algorithms will provide actionable insights to policymakers and small businesses through a self-serve analytics platform.

The result? Better crafted policy, more informed decisions, leading to economically and socially healthy communities.
